Welcome to the Explore Indonesia

Definition

Explore Indonesia is a travel community created to make it easier for tourists who want to find information about the place to be visited. You can join us on a journey of exploration together. besides that you can also ask to talk about all things related to travel ranging from costs, tours, food and activities. so this platform is perfect for you to get information before traveling.

History

The website explore Indonesia started in 2018. This website aims to make it easier for tourists to get information. Explore Indonesia is not only a website to get information, but on this website it also happens for us to add friends, take a stopover with willing group members, ask about travel costs to a place and travel together.
